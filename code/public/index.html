Welcome to Hollywood Movies

In this data visualization you can explore the top grossing 915 Hollywood movies between 1970 and 2021

Once you close this modal, you'll be able to view the data in a few different ways.

The starter screen displays all the movies over the years. While the Y-axis always represents the Box Office in millions of dollars, the X-axis might change. On the "All Years" screen it represents the years.

While remaining on the "All Years" screen, you can filter the movies by genre by clicking on one of the buttons on the right.

Initially the Box Office is not adjusted to inflation but you can do so by clicking the corresponding checkbox at the top right of the screen.

Finally you can view the movies by year by either scrolling or moving the vertical slider on the right. On this view, the X-axis represents the distributors responsible for the movie release.

Enjoy!
